About me

I am a 19-year-old and currently a freshman studying Computer Science at HCMUT

I have just been programming for 1 year since I got into University. Before that, I was a student specialised in Chemistry.

However, I soon realized I don’t have the passion to pursue higher education in Chemistry. And I didn’t really have much preference so I had a hard time when it came to choosing major. In the end, I decided that I want a complete change of direction which is, to me, is CS.

I found Computer Science to be incredibly intellectually challenging, stimulating, and rewarding. Though there have been some hard times, I am eager to challenge myself to do things I've never accomplished before and I strive to learn and improve on my skills every day.
